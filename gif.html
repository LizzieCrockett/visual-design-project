Animated GIF

• This GIF has been designed for use as a teaser for the project, in a similar use case to a museum or gallery event.
• It was created and animated in Photoshop.
• From the standard suggested sizes, I chose the "large rectangle" 336x280 as I needed it to be relatively square to display a portrait image, but not as tall as a 300x600 half page.
• I used the stylised image created for Image 4, the Lely portrait (Image 1), and the logo in PNG format.
• I have used a lot of opacity tweens to create a smooth effect between frames.
• I experimented with more position tweens, to "drop" words into the frame, for instance, but even with frames of 0.01 seconds, they appeared too jerky for the style of the project.
• The typeface for the Virginia Woolf quote is Bell MT. It contrasts with the typeface in the opening image, conveying a sense of modernity to help bring the idea of Aphra Behn into a modern context.
• For the "playwright, poet, spy" text, I chose a typewriter-style font, JMH Typewriter, to create a similar effect as in Image 6. In addition, typewriter-style typefaces to me convey a sense of spy stories, so this adds to the effect).
• Rather than use black for the background, I chose Eigengrau, #16161D, as it is a little less harsh on the eye than complete black.
• The GIF is saved for web with 256 colours, as the painting requires retaining as many colours as possible.
